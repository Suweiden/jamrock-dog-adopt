COVID-19 Pet Food Help

Pet food assistance is available to qualifying pet owners who first fill out a COVID-19 Pet Food Assistance Application. The Jamrock Dog Adopt is no longer offering a drive-through pet food bank.

Clinic Services

The Jamrock Dog Adopt Clinic provides affordable, high-quality spay/neuter surgeries for cats and dogs. Since 2007, the spay/neuter experts at the Jamrock Clinic have provided surgeries to more than 125,000 felines and canines!

Pet Surrender

Surrendering your pet to a sheltering agency should be considered a last resort. Please consider all available alternatives prior to considering surrendering. Our free Pet Help Line has many resources and referrals that may help you keep your pet. You may also want to consider rehoming your animal instead of placing them in a shelter.
